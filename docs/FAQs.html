SUPPORT / FAQ

Orders & Shipping

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also log into your account and view order status under "Order History". Delivery updates are sent in real time.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery takes 7–14 business days. Duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's customs policy.

What are the delivery charges?

Shipping is free on orders above PKR 3,500. For orders below that, a flat rate of PKR 199 applies for local and PKR 399 for national shipments.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, with tags attached. To initiate a return, visit our Returns Center or contact support.

How do I exchange an item?

Select the "Exchange" option on our returns portal. Choose the new size/color. If the item is available, we'll ship it after receiving the original. Price differences will be adjusted.

Can I return sale items?

Sale items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned. However, discounted items from regular collections are eligible for return within 7 days.

Sizing & Fit

How do I find my correct size?

We provide a detailed size guide on every product page. Measure your chest, waist, and hips and compare with our chart. If unsure, our customer support can help.

Are your clothes true to size?

Most of our pieces follow standard sizing, but some have an oversized fit (noted in the description). Check the "fit" notes on the product page for guidance.

What if I receive a wrong size?

Contact us immediately. We'll arrange a free exchange or provide a return label. Wrong items are replaced at no extra cost.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Our support team is ready to assist with any questions.
